Tai Hyoung (William) Rhee

I am currently an M.S. student in Mechanical Engineering at the RPM Robotics Lab, Seoul National University, advised by Prof. Ayoung Kim.

My research interests include Visual-Inertial Odometry (VIO), SLAM and 3D reconstruction, thermal infrared imaging, and IMU bias estimation.
